# Mage Knight Skills Reference

Source: [Unofficial Mage Knight Wiki](https://unofficialmageknighttheboardgame.fandom.com/wiki/Skills)

Skills are represented by Skill tokens unique to each hero. Players gain skills at even-numbered Fame levels (2, 4, 6, 8, 10).

**Total: 70 Skills** (10 per hero × 7 heroes)

## Skill Types

| Type | Icon | Usage |
|------|------|-------|
| **Once per Turn** | (none) | Can be used once each turn |
| **Once per Round** | Flip arrow | Flip token when used; cannot use again until next Round |
| **Interactive** | Special icon | Place in center; affects other players until your next turn |

---

## Norowas Skills

Norowas focuses on leadership, unit support, and day-time abilities.

| Skill | Type | Effect |
|-------|------|--------|
| **Forward March** | Once/Turn | Move 1 for each Ready and Unwounded Unit you control (max Move 3) |
| **Day Sharpshooting** | Once/Turn | Ranged Attack 2 (Day) or Ranged Attack 1 (Night) |
| **Inspiration** | Once/Round | Flip to Ready or Heal a Unit (except in combat) |
| **Bright Negotiation** | Once/Turn | Influence 3 (Day) or Influence 2 (Night) |
| **Leaves in the Wind** | Once/Round | Flip to gain 1 green crystal and 1 white mana token |
| **Whispers in the Treetops** | Once/Round | Flip to gain 1 white crystal and 1 green mana token |
| **Leadership** | Once/Turn | When activating a Unit: +3 Block, +2 Attack, or +1 Ranged Attack |
| **Bonds of Loyalty** | Special | Acts as Command token. Unit costs -5 Influence (min 0). Unit works even when Units normally can't. Cannot be disbanded. Adds 2 Regular Units to offer when acquired. |
| **Motivation (Norowas)** | Once/Round | On any player's turn: flip to draw 2 cards. If lowest Fame (not tied): +1 white mana. Can't use another Motivation until end of your next turn. |
| **Prayer of Weather** | Interactive | Place in center. Until your next turn: your terrain costs -2 (min 1), other players' terrain costs +1 (max 5). |

## Tovak Skills

Tovak focuses on physical combat, night-time abilities, and anti-magic.

| Skill | Type | Effect |
|-------|------|--------|
| **Double Time** | Once/Turn | Move 2 (Day) or Move 1 (Night) |
| **Night Sharpshooting** | Once/Turn | Ranged Attack 1 (Day) or Ranged Attack 2 (Night) |
| **Cold Swordsmanship** | Once/Turn | Attack 2 or Ice Attack 2 |
| **Shield Mastery** | Once/Turn | Block 3, or Fire Block 2, or Ice Block 2 |
| **Resistance Break** | Once/Turn | Target enemy: Armor -1 for each resistance it has (min 1) |
| **I Feel No Pain** | Once/Turn | Except in combat: Discard 1 Wound from hand, draw a card |
| **I Don't Give a Damn** | Once/Turn | One sideways card gives +2 instead of +1. Advanced Action/Spell/Artifact gives +3. |
| **Who Needs Magic?** | Once/Turn | One sideways card gives +2 instead of +1. If you use no die from Source this turn: +3 instead. |
| **Motivation (Tovak)** | Once/Round | On any player's turn: flip to draw 2 cards. If lowest Fame (not tied): +1 blue mana. Can't use another Motivation until end of your next turn. |
| **Mana Exploit** | Interactive | Choose non-gold mana color, gain that token. Place in center. Until your next turn: other players take Wounds when using mana of colors other than the marked color (1 Wound per color per player). |

## Goldyx Skills

Goldyx focuses on crystals, mana manipulation, and siege attacks.

| Skill | Type | Effect |
|-------|------|--------|
| **Freezing Power** | Once/Turn | Siege Attack 1 or Ice Siege Attack 1 |
| **Potion Making** | Once/Round | Flip for Heal 2 (except in combat) |
| **White Crystal Craft** | Once/Round | Flip to gain 1 blue crystal and 1 white mana token |
| **Green Crystal Craft** | Once/Round | Flip to gain 1 blue crystal and 1 green mana token |
| **Red Crystal Craft** | Once/Round | Flip to gain 1 blue crystal and 1 red mana token |
| **Glittering Fortune** | Once/Turn | During interaction: Influence 1 per different color crystal in Inventory (cannot use outside interaction) |
| **Flight** | Once/Round | Flip to move to adjacent space free, or move 2 spaces for 2 Move. Must end in safe space. Does not provoke. |
| **Universal Power** | Once/Turn | Add 1 mana to a sideways card: it gives +3 instead of +1. If Action/Spell of same color: +4. |
| **Motivation (Goldyx)** | Once/Round | On any player's turn: flip to draw 2 cards. If lowest Fame (not tied): +1 green mana. Can't use another Motivation until end of your next turn. |
| **Source Freeze** | Interactive | Place in Source. Other players cannot use their standard die. On your next turn: gain 1 crystal of any basic color in Source without rerolling. |

## Arythea Skills

Arythea focuses on dark magic, fire, wounds, and night-time abilities.

| Skill | Type | Effect |
|-------|------|--------|
| **Dark Paths** | Once/Turn | Move 1 (Day) or Move 2 (Night) |
| **Burning Power** | Once/Turn | Siege Attack 1 or Fire Siege Attack 1 |
| **Hot Swordsmanship** | Once/Turn | Attack 2 or Fire Attack 2 |
| **Dark Negotiation** | Once/Turn | Influence 2 (Day) or Influence 3 (Night) |
| **Dark Fire Magic** | Once/Round | Flip to gain 1 red crystal and 1 red or black mana token |
| **Power of Pain** | Once/Turn | Play 1 Wound sideways as non-Wound card: +2 instead of +1. Put Wound in discard at end of turn. |
| **Invocation** | Once/Turn | Discard Wound: gain red or black mana. Discard non-Wound: gain white or green mana. Must spend gained mana immediately. |
| **Polarization** | Once/Turn | Use 1 mana as opposite color. Day: black → any other. Night: gold → black. |
| **Motivation (Arythea)** | Once/Round | On any player's turn: flip to draw 2 cards. If lowest Fame (not tied): +1 red mana. Can't use another Motivation until end of your next turn. |
| **Healing Ritual** | Interactive | Flip (except combat): Discard up to 2 Wounds from hand. One goes to closest hero's hand instead of Wound pile. |

## Wolfhawk Skills

Wolfhawk focuses on ranged combat, hunting, and solo play.

| Skill | Type | Effect |
|-------|------|--------|
| **Refreshing Bath** | Once/Round | Flip for Heal 1 and 1 blue crystal (except combat) |
| **Refreshing Breeze** | Once/Round | Flip for Heal 1 and 1 white crystal (except combat) |
| **Hawk Eyes** | Once/Turn | Move 1. Night: exploring costs -1 Move. Day: reveal garrisons at distance 2. |
| **On Her Own** | Once/Turn | Influence 1. Influence 3 instead if you recruit no Unit for Influence this turn. |
| **Deadly Aim** | Once/Turn | Ranged/Siege phase: +1 to any Attack card. Attack phase: +2 to any Attack card (including sideways). |
| **Know Your Prey** | Once/Round | During combat: flip to ignore one enemy ability, or remove one element from enemy attack. Cannot use vs magic immune. |
| **Taunt** | Once/Turn | Block phase: Reduce enemy attack by 1, OR increase enemy attack by 2 and reduce its Armor by 2 (min 1). |
| **Dueling** | Once/Turn | Block phase: Block 1 and Attack 1 vs same enemy in Attack phase. +1 Fame if you defeat it without using any Unit abilities. |
| **Motivation (Wolfhawk)** | Once/Round | On any player's turn: flip to draw 2 cards. If lowest Fame (not tied): +1 Fame. Can't use another Motivation until end of your next turn. |
| **Wolf's Howl** | Interactive | Once/Round (except interaction): One sideways card gives +4. +1 more per Command token with no Unit. Place in center: until your next turn, other players' Units get -1 to all Attacks and Blocks. |

## Krang Skills

Krang focuses on chaos, enemy manipulation, and unconventional tactics.

| Skill | Type | Effect |
|-------|------|--------|
| **Spirit Guides** | Once/Turn | Move 1 and may add +1 to a Block in Block phase |
| **Battle Hardened** | Once/Turn | Ignore next 2 damage from single physical Attack or 1 damage from non-physical Attack |
| **Battle Frenzy** | Once/Turn | Attack 2. Flip for Attack 4 instead. Flip back when resting. |
| **Shamanic Ritual** | Once/Round | Flip to gain mana token of any color. May flip back as your action for the turn. |
| **Regenerate** | Once/Turn | Pay mana of any color, discard Wound. If red mana or lowest Fame: also draw a card. |
| **Arcane Disguise** | Once/Turn | Influence 2, or flip to ignore reputation effects this turn. Flip back by paying green mana. |
| **Puppet Master** | Once/Turn | Keep 1 defeated enemy token, or discard kept token for: Attack at half its Attack (rounded up) or Block at half its Armor (rounded up). Elements transfer. |
| **Master of Chaos** | Once/Turn | Rotate shield clockwise to next color for: Blue=Block 3, Green=Move 1, Black=Cold Fire Ranged Attack 1, White=Influence 2, Red=Attack 2, Gold=choose any. |
| **Curse** | Once/Turn | Enemy Attack -1 or enemy Armor -1 (min 1). Cannot use on fortified enemy in Ranged/Siege phase. |
| **Mana Suppression** | Interactive | Place in center. Until your next turn: first basic mana used each player's turn costs extra mana of that color (goes on token). Then gain crystal matching one token color. |

## Braevalar Skills

Braevalar focuses on nature, elemental magic, and terrain mastery.

| Skill | Type | Effect |
|-------|------|--------|
| **Elemental Resistance** | Once/Turn | Ignore next 2 damage from Fire or Ice Attack, or 1 damage from other Attack type |
| **Feral Allies** | Once/Turn | Exploring costs -1 Move. Attack 1 or reduce enemy attack by 1. |
| **Thunderstorm** | Once/Round | Flip to gain 1 green or blue mana and 1 green or white mana |
| **Lightning Storm** | Once/Round | Flip to gain 1 blue or green mana and 1 blue or red mana |
| **Beguile** | Once/Turn | Influence 3. Influence 2 at fortified site. Influence 4 at Magical Glade. |
| **Forked Lightning** | Once/Turn | Ranged Cold Fire Attack 1 against up to 3 different enemies |
| **Shapeshift** | Once/Turn | One Basic Action with fixed Move/Attack/Block instead gives same amount of one of the other two. Elements preserved. |
| **Secret Ways** | Once/Turn | Move 1. May enter mountains for 5 Move (safe space). Pay blue mana: may enter lakes for 2 Move (safe at end of turn). |
| **Regenerate** | Once/Turn | Pay mana of any color, discard Wound. If red mana or lowest Fame: also draw a card. |
| **Nature's Vengeance** | Interactive | Once/Round: reduce enemy attack by 1, that enemy gains Cumbersome. Place in center: until your next turn, all other enemies get +1 attack in Block phase. |

## Notes

- Each hero has 10 unique skills (70 total)
- **Motivation** appears on all heroes but grants different colored mana based on the hero
- **Regenerate** appears on both Krang and Braevalar with identical effects
- Interactive skills affect all players and are placed in the center of the table
- Skills cannot be used more than once per turn (even "once per turn" skills)
- Flip-based skills remain face-down until the start of the next Round
